Prashanth Neel, at last graced the big screens worldwide today amid towering expectations.
Join us as we delve into our review to determine whether the film roars like a dinosaur or not.

    Review:

  • Story

    • Deva (Prabhas) lives a casual life with a shadowy past along with his mother in Tinsukia.
    The tranquility is disrupted when a group of goons searches for Aadhya (Shruti Haasan),
    newly arrived in town.Deva tracks the gang and discovers it’s the scheme of his best friend
    Varadharaja Mannar (Prithviraj Sukumaran) in Khansaar city. The war escalates,
    posing serious questions. Who is Aadhya? What ties her to Varadharaj? What caused the
    enmity between the two best friends? The movie unfolds the answers.
